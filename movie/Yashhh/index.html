I-RIS IRL Show YASHHH

"The John Mayer meets Alan Thicke of India"

YASHMEETH — known as YASHHH — is an aspiring Indian pop star whose soulful musical style and cool, laid-back appearance have drawn comparisons to John Mayer's guitar-driven artistry and Alan Thicke's effortless charisma. His IRL show follows him behind the scenes as he navigates his personal life, performs at concerts and venues across India, and manages the intense pressure of bidding wars between multiple record labels — all vying for the rights to sign the next big thing in Indian pop music.
